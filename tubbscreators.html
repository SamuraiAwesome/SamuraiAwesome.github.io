About Us

The Tubbs Force was created by two 10-year-old kids: Sam and Cole. They came up with the idea based on an imaginary cat that rode to swim lessons with them on the roof of their car. Later on a third member was added to the team: Riley,an accomplished coder who was given access to the website in its creation stage. More members were added, including Noah, an accomplished young artist, Remy, and Luke, who has now quit the team. Later on, two other coders, Jack and Henry, joined and were given access to the website. The team is now recruiting more members.
